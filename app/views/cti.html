How to open and use this app

  1. Run fdk run and open Freshdesk with ?dev=true
  2. Look at the bottom-left corner of Freshdesk for the phone / app icon
  3. Click that icon to expand the CTI panel (400×400px widget)
  4. On any ticket, click a phone number or call icon in Contact details
  5. The number appears below — this is separate from the ticket sidebar app
